# Join FABILIS

We are always looking for talented and motivated individuals to join the FABILIS lab. If you are interested in contributing to groundbreaking research in ionic liquid ion sources, we would love to hear from you. To apply, please send your CV and a brief statement of interest to Dr. Carla Perez-Martinez at carlaperezmartinez@ucl.ac.uk.
